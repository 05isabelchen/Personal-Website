Get to know me!

Background

Hello! I'm Isabel Chen, currently a second year at University of California, Berkeley studying Economics and Data Science. My academic journey has been driven by my passion for understanding economic patterns and leveraging data to derive meaningful insights.

I grew up in Frederick, Maryland and I developed an early interest in economics when I was in high school. I really love the application of economics to improve policies and industries and how data science can be used to enhance research.

When I'm not analyzing data or studying economic theories, you can find me reading, watching movies, or discovering a new hobby.

Academic Interests

Economics

My favorite aspects of economics include econometrics, macroeconomic policy, and financial economics. I fell in love with economics when I was in high school during my AP Macroeconomics class. My teacher was amazing and made me really love economics in the end.

Data Science

In data science, I'm drawn to specific aspects like machine learning, data visualization, statistical modeling, etc. I love how data science has that interconnection with economics and how it can be used to study eocnomic theories and policies. Data science is such an amazing tool that can be used to enhance an aspect or field.
